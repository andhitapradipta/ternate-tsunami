How to prepare

This page is a part of a MSc thesis and is not meant to be used as an official advice.

Maluku has been declared one of the tsunami risk zones in Indonesia. Even though it is a very rare event, consequences can be devastating. There are a few things you can do upfront to be more ready for a tsunami.

Learn what a tsunami is

Understanding what causes a tsunami and how it works can help you notice early signs of it and help you evaluate your situation better.

Get tsunami alerts to your phone

Get notified via a text message if a tsunami alert is issued in Maluku area. Learning about a hazzard as early as possible can save your life.

Building a tsunami-resistant building

Location

The most important aspect of building a tsunami-resitant building is choosing its location.
Learn what are the dos and don'ts when choosing the building location.

Avoid lower ground

Avoid locating the building in the low-lying areas.

Hindari membangun rumah didataran rendah atau celukan.

Title

Where possible, try to build on higher ground.

Usahakan membangun didataran yang lebih tinggi.

Title

If higher ground is not available, build the building on a stilts structure. Watch the video to see the demonstration of how stilts help to avoid damage to the main structure.

Ketika dataran tinggi tidak tersedia, bangunan sebaiknya dibangun dengan struktur panggung.

Title

Construct the building at least 1 meter away from the top slope and 1 meter away from the cut; the retaining wall is necessary to support the slope from the landslide.

Bangunan setidaknya berjarak 1 meter dari tepian lereng dan 1 meter dari jurang; dinding penyangga diperlukan untuk menjaga dari bahaya tanah longsor.

Plan and Form of the Building

Robustness of the overall structure depends on the plan and form of the building. It is important to understand that tsunamis are often preceded by earthquakes, that can weaken otherwise tsunami-resistant structures.

Title

Building with a symmetrical plan is safer than an asymmetrical plan building. It is recommended to divide an asymetric building into a number of symmetric units.

Bangunan dengan denah yang simetri lebih aman dibandingan dengan denah assimetri. Disarankan untuk membagi bangunan menjadi beberapa bagian dengan bentuk simetri.

Title

Avoid making buildings with "C", "H" and "T" shaped plans in disaster prone areas

Hindari membuat bangunan dengan bentuk denah C H T didaerah rawan bencana.

Title

A square-plan building is safer than a long, rectangular building with walls longer than 7 metres.

Bangunan berbentuk persegi lebih aman dibandingkan bangunan yang berbentuk persegi panjang dengan dinding lebih panjang dari 7m.

Title

A house with a porch on one side of the corner can easily get damaged in comparisson to a house that has a symmetrical porch located in the centre.

Rumah dengan beranda samping lebih mudah rusak dibandingkan dengan rumah dengan beranda yang berada ditengah atau simetris dikedua sisi.

Title

A long building can get damaged easier and is more likely to collapse than a shorter building. Avoid constructing a house with the wall length more than 3 times its width. If this requirement can not be satsified, it is recommended to divide the building into two units.

Bangunan berbentuk panjang lebih mudah rusak atau roboh dibandingkan dengan bangunan yang lebih pendek. Hindari membangun rumah dengan panjang lebih dari tiga kali lebarnya, disarankan untuk membagi bangunan menjadi dua unit terpisah.

Title

A roof with four slopes provides a more resitant structure than a roof with two slopes.

Disarankan untuk membangun rumah dengan atap berbentuk limas daripada perisai karena bentuk limas lebih kuat dan stabil.

Title

For pitched roofs, the slope should be between 22 to 30 degrees and the roof overhang must be around 0.5 metre to protect the walls from erosion cause due to frequent heavy rain in Indonesia.

Kemiringan atap sebaiknya antara 22 sampai 30 derajat, sedangkan perpanjangan sebaiknya sekitar 0.5m dikarenakan Indonesia memiliki curah hujan yang tinggi.

Wall Design

Title

Walls longer than 7 metres can collapse easily, make it stronger by constructing cross walls to securely connect the long walls.

Dinding yang lebih panjang dari 7m bisa roboh dengan mudah apabila tidak dikuatkan dengan membangun dinding dalam.

Title

Partition walls built after the outer wall construction can collapse easily.

Dinding partisi yang dibangun ketika dinding luar sudah selesai dibangun lebih rentan roboh.

Title

Build partition walls simultaneously with other walls.

Dinding partisi harus dibangun bersama dengan seluruh dinding bangunan.

Title

It is recommended to build walls that have sufficient thickness (around 15-23cm).

Disarankan untuk membangun dinding yang memiliki ketebalan cukup (15-23cm) daripada dinding yang tipis dan tinggi.

Title

A tall masonry parapet wall is more likely to collapse compared to a low parapet extended by a metal railing.

Dinding tembok pembatas dengan material bata lebih mudah roboh dibandingkan dengan dinding tembok yang rendah dan diperkuat dengan susuran dari bahan logam.

Title

In load bearing structures it is not recommended to use thin brick walls (<150mm), since they can not resist forces cause by tsunamis and earthquakes.

Dalam struktur bantalan beban, tidak dianjurkan untuk menggunakan dinding bata tipis (<150mm), karena lebih lemah dalam menghadapi tsunami / gempa di daerah rawan bencana.

Title

High load walls on sloping roofs are likely to collapse during a tsunami, construct it no higher than 230mm and strengthen it with TOR reinforcing bar inside.

Dinding pada atap lebih mudah runtuh, sama seperti dinding tembok pembatas. Usahakan membangunnya tidak lebih tinggi dari 230mm dan diperkuat dengan besi baja tulangan dalam, sehingga dinding atap lebih kokoh.

Title

Gable walls should have around 1 metre height above eave level (eavesÂ are the edges of the roof that overhang the face of a wall and, normally, project beyond the side of a building). If the design is taller than 1 metre, it is safer to use light materials such are CGI (corrugated galvanized iron) sheets or timber planks.

Dinding atap pelana sebaiknya memiliki ketinggian sekitar 1m di atas permukaan atap. Jika desain dinding lebih tinggi dari 1m, lebih aman untuk menggunakan bahan yang ringan seperti papan seng atau papan kayu.
